Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if career coaching is right for me?

Career coaching is ideal if you're feeling stuck, unfulfilled, or uncertain about your next career move. If you're a high performer looking to make meaningful change without starting over, our approach is designed for you.

What happens during the free consultation?

We'll discuss your current situation, career goals, and challenges. You'll learn about our coaching approach and we'll explore which services might be the best fit for your needs. There's no pressure or obligation.

Do you work with people in all industries?

Yes! Our coaches have experience across multiple industries and our proven methodology works regardless of your field. We adapt our approach to your specific industry and career level.

How long does the coaching process typically take?

It varies based on your goals and chosen program. Career discovery typically takes 6-8 weeks, while career transitions can take 3-6 months. We'll create a timeline that works for your situation.

Is coaching conducted online or in person?

All our coaching is conducted online via video calls, making it convenient and accessible regardless of your location. We serve clients nationwide.

What if I'm not sure what I want to do next?

That's exactly what we help with! Our career discovery process is designed to help you gain clarity on your direction, even if you have no idea where to start.
