üí° Pro Testing Tips

Test early and often: Check responsiveness as you build each component, not just at the end. Use real content: Test with actual text and images, not just placeholder content. Consider touch targets: Ensure interactive elements are at least 44px for comfortable tapping. Check in landscape mode: Don't forget to test both portrait and landscape orientations.
